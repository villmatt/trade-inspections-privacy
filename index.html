Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 2026

Trade Inspections Pro ("the App") respects your privacy. We collect account data (email) via Supabase and document data (inspection findings, photos, and job addresses) solely for the purpose of generating your professional reports.

Data Handling

  • Local Cache: When using "Offline Mode," data is stored locally on your device.
  • Cloud Sync: Data is encrypted and synced to our secure Supabase backend.
  • Permissions: We request Camera and Photo access only to allow you to document site findings.

We do not share your inspection data or client information with third parties.
